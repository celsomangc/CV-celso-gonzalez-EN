Cover letter

Dear Mistral Team,

I am applying for the Systems Engineer position focused on infrastructure and large-scale Linux environments. My background is in computational science, but my day-to-day work revolves around executing, optimizing, and troubleshooting compute-intensive jobs on HPC systems, closely matching the challenges described in this role.

At Universidad Autónoma de Madrid, I work extensively with large-scale Linux clusters, including Tier-0 infrastructure such as MareNostrum 5 at the Barcelona Supercomputing Center. I regularly submit and monitor jobs through SLURM, diagnose failures, and improve performance and resource efficiency in multi-user environments. This has given me a strong practical understanding of how applications interact with underlying systems, particularly regarding scheduling, memory usage, and I/O behavior.

A central part of my work is improving the efficiency and reliability of computational workflows. I automate execution processes using Python, Fortran, and Bash, optimize parallel applications with MPI and OpenMP, and resolve issues related to software environments and dependencies. This requires not only technical problem-solving, but also close collaboration with researchers and HPC support teams to align computational demands with infrastructure constraints.

My experience working under real constraints in shared HPC environments, debugging complex issues and improving efficiency, has naturally driven my interest toward roles at the intersection of users and infrastructure, where performance, reliability, and system-level thinking are essential. In this regard, Mistral’s focus on large-scale AI infrastructure makes it an ideal environment for this transition from scientific computing to production systems, and the kind of place where I am keen to continue developing my career.

Thank you for your consideration.
